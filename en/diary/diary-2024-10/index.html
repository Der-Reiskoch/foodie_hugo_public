Foodie Diary (October 2024)

My culinary adventures

Jens (Der Reiskoch) 2024-10-08Diary DE/ EN

Gaeng Massaman Gai (10-08)

Despite a stressful day at work, I prepared a Thai Massaman Curry with chicken, or “Gaeng Massaman Gai” (แกงมัสมั่นไก่) in Thai, for lunch today.

Kuay Tiaw Moo Toon (10-07)

With the gloomy weather outside, I decided to warm up with a bowl of soup made with the bone broth I cooked up this weekend. I made a Thai pork noodle soup called “Kuay Tiaw Moo Toon” (ก๋วยเตี๋ยวหมูตุ๋น), flavored with star anise and cinnamon.

Gaeng Buan (10-04)

Today, I harvested the leaves from our lemongrass plant and used them to make a Thai “Gaeng Buan” (แกงบวน) following Ying’s recipe. Funny, when I took her cooking class on Koh Samui, I thought I could never cook this at home because we don’t have lemongrass leaves here.

Two times Khao Soi (10-03)

I decided to try my hand at two different versions of the Northern Thai noodle soup “Khao Soi”. I made both “Khao Soi Nuea” (ข้าวซอยเนื้อ) and “Khao Soi Nam Naa” (ข้าวซอยน้ำหน้า), using recipes from Andy Ricker’s book “Pok Pok Noodles”. Even though they have the same name, they are quite distinct from each other.

Recommended Pok Pok Noodles at amazon.de*

Pad Ga Prao Gai (10-02)

For lunch today, I had a quick but spicy “Pad Ga Prao Gai” (ผัดกะเพราไก่) with rice and a sunny-side-up egg.
